📞 Step-by-Step Guide to Setting Up a Phone Number & AI Assistant in VAPI
🚀 In this guide, you'll learn how to create a phone number in VAPI and set up an AI assistant that can handle calls automatically. Perfect for businesses, customer support, or AI-driven voice assistants! 🎙️🤖

🔹 Step 1: Sign Up & Log in to VAPI
1️⃣ Go to VAPI's website
2️⃣ Sign up for an account (or log in if you already have one).
3️⃣ Verify your email to activate your account.

✅ Now you're ready to create a virtual AI-powered phone assistant!

🔹 Step 2: Get a Virtual Phone Number
1️⃣ Go to the "Phone Numbers" section in VAPI.
2️⃣ Click "Add New Number" → Choose your preferred country/region.
3️⃣ Select a phone number from the list of available numbers.
4️⃣ Click "Confirm" to assign the number to your account.

✅ Your AI assistant now has a dedicated phone number! 📞

🔹 Step 3: Create a New AI Assistant
1️⃣ Navigate to the "Assistants" section in VAPI.
2️⃣ Click "Create New Assistant".
3️⃣ Enter a Name → Example: "Varshini - Restaurant AI".
4️⃣ Select a Voice Profile → Choose an AI voice (e.g., female/male, Indian accent, etc.).
5️⃣ Define the Assistant’s Role:

Example: "You are a restaurant reservation assistant. Your job is to take customer booking requests and confirm available slots."
6️⃣ Click "Save" to create your assistant.
✅ Now, your AI assistant is ready to handle customer calls! 🎙️📞

🔹 Step 4: Connect the Assistant to the Phone Number
1️⃣ Go to "Phone Numbers" and select your number.
2️⃣ Click "Assign Assistant" and select your AI assistant.
3️⃣ Set up "Default Behavior":

Choose whether the assistant answers all calls or only specific ones.
4️⃣ Click "Save" to finalize the setup.
✅ Your assistant is now linked to the phone number and will answer calls automatically! 🎉

🔹 Step 5: Customize Your Assistant’s Responses (VAPI Prompting)
Go to your Assistant settings and define the system prompt.

✅ Now, your AI assistant can have intelligent, structured conversations! 🗣️

🔹 Step 6: Test Your AI Assistant
1️⃣ Call your assigned phone number using any phone.
2️⃣ Speak to the AI assistant and check how it responds.
3️⃣ Ensure it follows the conversation flow correctly (e.g., taking reservations, answering FAQs).
4️⃣ Make adjustments in the Assistant Settings if needed.

✅ Your AI phone assistant is live and ready to serve customers! 🎤🎉
